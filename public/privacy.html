Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026
Cloud Panel is self-hosted, open-source software. This policy describes how this instance handles your data. The person/team running it (the "Operator") controls the server and is the data controller. Operators should adapt this template to their setup and local laws.

1. Information we collect

  • Account info: username, email address, optional name, and a securely hashed password (never stored in plain text).
  • Server content: the files, configurations, and data you upload to your servers and backups.
  • Economy data: your coin balance, resource quotas, and shop/AFK activity.
  • Activity logs: actions such as sign-ins, server power actions, purchases, and moderation events.
  • Technical data: your IP address (used for security, rate-limiting, and abuse prevention) and basic request information.

2. How we use it

  • To provide and operate the Service (accounts, servers, backups, economy).
  • To keep the Service secure — preventing abuse, brute-force attempts, and fraud.
  • To provide support and respond to your requests.

3. Cookies & local storage

The panel stores a login token in your browser's local storage to keep you signed in. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

4. Sharing & third parties

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • Your data stays on the Operator's server, except where sharing is required by law or necessary to run the Service.
  • When you install certain server types ("eggs"), the panel downloads files from third-party providers (e.g. PaperMC, Mojang/Minecraft, Fabric). Those requests are handled by those providers under their own policies.
  • The hosting provider/VPS that runs this instance may process data as part of normal operation.

5. Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. When your account is deleted, your personal data, servers, and backups are removed (some logs may be retained briefly for security and legal reasons).

6. Security

We use measures including bcrypt password hashing, signed session tokens (JWT), rate-limiting, and path/access protections. No system is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your rights

You can view and update your account details in your account settings, and request access to or deletion of your data by contacting the Operator. Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or CCPA.

8. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum digital age in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from them.

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact the Operator via the community Discord.
